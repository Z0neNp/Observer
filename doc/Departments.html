Overview

Unites three main responsibilities of Observer: intelligence, interpretation, data, cooperation between previous three and View controllers, i.e. FriendlyResourcesController. Each responsibility has its own module:

  • Analysis - handles interpretation of intelligence.

  • Archive - handles data.

  • Intelligence - handles intelligence.

  • Shared - holds methods or values common to a group of departments.

  • ThinkTank - a bridge between Intelligence, Analysis and View controllers.
